# MetaHuman Adopted Direction

Adopted reference image:

`apps/avatar_3d_vrm/production/reference/misato_style_avatar_sheet_latest.png`

## Direction

Use this as the main visual direction for the next 3D avatar pass.

Create an original Chibaten desktop secretary avatar. Do not recreate the exact copyrighted character.

## Key Look

- Adult, confident, friendly secretary presence.
- Dark purple semilong hair with soft waves and face-framing bangs.
- Red cropped jacket with a sharp collar.
- Black fitted dress or black high-neck top plus skirt.
- Black ankle boots.
- Small earrings and simple chest accessory.
- Clean anime-real 3D look.
- Front, side, and back checks are required.

## Expressions

Required expressions:

- normal
- smile
- serious
- surprised
- wink
- talking mouth shapes
- blink

## MetaHuman Goal

MetaHuman is useful for realistic face quality and stable facial animation.

First target:

1. Create a MetaHuman base character.
2. Match hair color, face age, eye color, and mature expression balance.
3. Apply the closest MetaHuman long hair first.
4. Use Unreal clothing options as a temporary black base.
5. Model the red cropped jacket, black dress/skirt, boots, cross necklace, and earrings separately in Blender/Unreal if needed.
6. Keep the existing VRoid/VRM route as backup for desktop display.

## Desktop Goal

The final desktop avatar should feel natural:

- no random shaking
- no floating bob motion
- smooth expression switching
- natural blink
- natural small head and eye movement
- speech with mouth movement
